Overview

Maponian is one of the larger dialects of toki pona. Maponian is largely characterised by two things:

  1. Maponian is mostly taught through #pana-sona by a somewhat homogeneous speaker base that generally doesn’t have conflicts when trying to communicate through toki pona, resulting in a fairly standardised dialect of toki pona that incubates new speakers fast.

  2. Maponian has a faster than normal cycle of evolution due to the #sona-musi channel encouraging sharing and discussion of ideas that would otherwise wither on the vine or get shunned by the community.
